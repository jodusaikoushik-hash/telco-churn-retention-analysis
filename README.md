# 📞 Telco Customer Retention: A Strategic Deep Dive

## 📈 The Business Problem
Customer churn is costing the business **$1.4M** in annual recurring revenue. My objective was to find where the "revenue leak" is happening and identify high-risk customer profiles.

---

## 🔍 Top 3 Discovery Insights
1. **The $1.4M Impact:** Churn isn't just a number; it is a major financial drain on the company's bottom line.
2. **Contract Type is Key:** Customers on Month-to-month plans are **14x more likely** to leave than those on long-term contracts.
3. **The Fiber Optic Issue:** Fiber Optic users have a churn rate of **42%**, suggesting a need to investigate service quality or pricing models.
